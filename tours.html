Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to the tour?

Please bring a valid photo ID showing you are 21 or older. We recommend wearing comfortable shoes as the tour involves walking and standing. You may also want to bring a camera to capture the beautiful distillery.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have free parking available on-site for all tour guests. If you're using a rideshare service, please use our main entrance for drop-off and pick-up.

Are tours suitable for children?

While we welcome visitors of all ages, our tours are designed for adults 21 and over. Children must be accompanied by an adult at all times, and please note that only guests 21+ will be served alcohol during tastings.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand plans change. You can cancel or reschedule your tour up to 24 hours in advance for a full refund. For groups of 6 or more, we require 48 hours' notice for cancellations or changes.

Do you offer tours for people with disabilities?

Yes, our facility is wheelchair accessible, and we're happy to accommodate guests with disabilities. Please let us know in advance if you have any specific needs so we can ensure you have the best experience possible.
