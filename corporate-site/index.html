Our Vermont Heritage

For over 150 years, Bolton's has been a proud member of the Vermont brewing community. What began as a small family operation has grown into a cooperative of passionate brewers and distillers, all dedicated to crafting exceptional beverages that celebrate our state's rich maple heritage.
