Our Maple-Infused Beers

Discover the perfect balance of traditional brewing and Vermont's finest maple syrup

Crafted with Vermont's Finest Maple

Each of our beers is brewed with locally-sourced maple syrup, adding a unique Vermont twist to classic styles.

Brewer's Choice

Maple Amber Ale

5.8% ABV 25 IBU

A smooth, well-balanced amber ale with a rich malt character and subtle maple sweetness that lingers on the palate.

Tasting Notes

  • Appearance: Deep amber with off-white head
  • Aroma: Caramel, toasted malt, maple
  • Flavor: Rich malt, toffee, maple
  • Mouthfeel: Medium-bodied, crisp
